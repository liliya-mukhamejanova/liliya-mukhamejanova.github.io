Media of the City

The history of the press of the city dates back to 1867, when the first issue of the newspaper "Ural Military Gazette" was published in Uralsk. Later, "Uralets", "Yaitskaya Volya", the Turkic-language newspapers "Fiker", "Alash" were printed. As of 2019, the following newspapers are published: Uralskaya Nedelya, Informbirzha, MY CITY, Oral Oniri, ZhAYYK YNI, Vremya, Pulse of the City, Urals, Nadezhda, A week". There are radios: Avtoradio, Radio NS, Talap FM, Dacha, Retro FM, Russian Radio, Europe Plus.
